Bookshelves can hold 0 to many books.
A book must be on a book shelf.
Each book must have a title and ISBN number.
Bookshelves might have dimensions - length, depth and height.
Books may have dimensions height, width and depth so that it can be determined if they fit on a shelf.

Books can have 1 to many editions or copies.
